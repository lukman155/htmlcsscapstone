Yochai Benkler

Berkman Professor of Entrepreneeurial Legal Studies at Harvard Law School

Yochai Benkler is a Professor of Law at Harvard Law School and a Professor of Economics at Harvard University. He is the author of The Wealth of Networks: How Social Production Transforms Markets and Freedom (Yale University Press, 2006), which won the 2006 PROSE Award for the best book in law and economics, and The Penguin and the Leviathan: How Cooperation Triumphs over Self-Interest (Yale University Press, 2010).
